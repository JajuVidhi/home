  1. Romance

    Romantic movies are extremely popular in the Bollywood industry, and it is said that the industry has created some of the best love movies for viewers. There are some light-hearted and some with serious content, and many enjoy both types of movies. It is said some love stories in Bollywood movies bring a different point of view to love. So, here are some popular romantic movies...

    • Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani (2013)






      This movie is based on two individuals who meet each other at important times in their lives and eventually fall in love. This movie is known for the chemistry of the two actors, Ranbir Kapoor and Deepika Padukone, the songs, and the storyline.






      Source: Imdb.com
      (https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2178470/)


    • Khoobsurat (2014)






      This movie is based on the story of a physiotherapist, played by Sonam Kapoor, whose job it is to help a royal with his treatment. However, when she meets his son, played by Fawad Khan, she eventually falls in love with him. The movie is popular because it displays love between two opposite people.






      Source: Imdb.com
      (https://www.imdb.com/title/tt3554418/)


    • Jab We Met (2007)






      This movie is based on a chatty, extrovert girl, played by Kareena Kapoor, who meets a depressed, introverted guy, played by Shahid Kapoor, on a train. They both go together on a journey to Bhatinda, and later on, eventually, they both fall in love.






      Source: Gadgets360.com
      (https://www.gadgets360.com/entertainment/jab-we-met-movie-115947)


  2. Horror

    Horror movies in Bollywood are known to have engaging plots that keep viewers interested till the end. In addition, they are known to be full of imagination, allowing movies to have unique and creative storylines.

    • Bhool Bhulaiyaa (2007)






      This movie is based on a couple arriving from abroad and deciding to stay in their ancestral home. However, the house is haunted by an evil spirit, Manjulika. It is popular due to the themes of comedy and horror present in the film.







      Source: Imdb.com
      (https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0995031/)


    • Stree (2018)






      This movie is based on a legend where a female spirit stalks and abducts men at night. Hence, men walk in groups, thinking they might be able to protect themselves, but do they succeed and find out the motive of the spirit?







      Source: Sacnilk.com
      (https://www.sacnilk.com/articles/entertainment/box_office/Stree_Day_Wise_and_Total_Box_Office_Collection?hl=en)


    • Bhediya (2022)






      This movie is based on the story of a man who transforms into a werewolf when he gets bit by a wolf. When he tries to find out the answers to his questions, he goes on an adventure through many twists and turns.







      Source: Koimoi.com
      (https://www.koimoi.com/bollywood-movies/movie-reviews/bhediya-movie-review-youll-come-in-for-varun-dhawan-but-will-stay-back-for-abhishek-banerjee/)


  3. Comedy

    Comedy movies are also popular in the Bollywood industry. Many are ones you can watch with your family and friends and have a good laugh.

    • Mubarakan (2017)






      This movie is a comedy about two twin brothers getting married to their lovers at the same time, but due to miscommunication, it is a whole journey of laughter.







      Source: Indiaforums.com
      (https://www.indiaforums.com/article/movie-review-mubarakan_114480)


    • Hera Pheri (2000)






      This movie is based on a trio that faces many ups and downs and ends up engaging in dangerous activities due to greed.







      Source: Indianexpress.com
      (https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/hera-pheri-producer-alleges-priyadarshan-left-the-film-midway-7451959/)


    • 3 Idiots (2009)






      This movie is a classic comedy about two friends looking for the last idiot to join their group, and the three go on a fun adventure through college and the rest of their lives.
