Types of Bollywood Songs

  1. Romantic

    Bollywood romance songs always give happy, positive vibes to listeners, lighting up their day. In Bollywood, romantic songs are known by many and generally have soft, melodic tunes.

    • Mast Magan (2 States)






      Mast Magan is a song from the movie, 2 States, and is sung by Arijit Singh and Chinmayi Sripada. The song features the actors, Arjun Kapoor and Alia Bhatt.








    • Tum Se Hi (Jab We Met)






      Tum Se Hi is a song from the movie, Jab We Met, and is sung by Mohit Chauhan. The song features the actors, Shahid Kapoor and Kareena Kapoor.








    • Apna Bana Le (Bhediya)






      Apna Bana Le is a song from the movie, Bhediya, and is sung by Arjit Singh and Sachin-Jigar. The song features the actors, Varun Dhawan and Kriti Sanon.








  2. Heartbreak

    Bollywood also has outstanding heartbreak songs. Each line is normally relatable, so when you are having a tough time, these songs can provide you with comfort.

    • Agar Tum Saath Ho (Tamasha)






      Agar Tum Saath Ho is a song from the movie, Tamasha, and is sung by Arijit Singh and Alka Yagnik. The song features the actors, Ranbir Kapoor and Deepika Padukone.





    • Phir Bhi Tumko Chahunga (Half Girlfriend)






      Phir Bhi Tumko Chahunga is a song from the movie, Half Girlfriend, and is sung by Arijit Singh and Shashaa Tirupati. The song features the actors, Arjun Kapoor and Shraddha Kapoor.





    • Channa Mereya (Ae Dil Hai Mushkil)






      Channa Mereya is a song from the movie, Ae Dil Hai Mushkil, and is sung by Arijit Singh. The song features the actors, Ranbir Kapoor and Anushka Sharma.








  3. Party

    Dance parties need songs, and Bollywood party songs bring energetic vibes, are catchy, and will make anyone show off their dance moves in front of everyone.

    • Aankh Marey (Simmba)






      Aankh Marey is a song from the movie, Simmba, and is sung by Neha Kakkar, Mika Singh And Kumar Sanu. The song features the actors, Ranveer Singh and Sara Ali Khan.







    • Kala Chashma (Baar Baar Dekho)






      Kala Chashma is a song from the movie, Baar Baar Dekho, and is sung by Amar Arshi, Badshah And Neha Kakkar. The song features the actors, Katrina Kaif and Sidharth Malhotra.






    • Disco Disco (A Gentleman)






      Disco Disco is a song from the movie, A Gentleman, and is sung by Benny Dayal and Shirley Setia. The song features the actors, Sidharth Malhotra and Jacqueline Fernandez.
