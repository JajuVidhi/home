What is Bollywood?

Bollywood is an industry of movies, mainly in the Hindi language. This industry produces the majority of films for the population of India. Bollywood music is from these movies; hence, we have a page about Bollywood songs. It is also the largest film industry in the world, producing lots of content every year. The name Bollywood is a version of Hollywood; however, it also comes from Mumbai (named Bombay before), one of the lively cities of India. Hence, the name Bollywood comes from the city named Bombay and Hollywood.
