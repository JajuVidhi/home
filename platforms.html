Finding Bollywood movies on streaming platforms can sometimes be hard. Here are some platforms you can use!

  1. Disney+ Hotstar






    Disney+ Hotstar has a wide range of Bollywood movies and is a popular streaming platform. It has movies in multiple languages of India and is an extremely organized website. Besides movies, they have Indian TV shows and sports based content as well.






    Source: Anish K.
    Keralatv.in
    (https://www.keralatv.in/disney-hotstar-app/)


  2. Zee5






    Zee5 also has many Bollywood movies on its platform. The site has some old gems but also some modern Bollywood content for viewers. It is said the platform barely has any lag, and the platform is generally user-friendly.







    Source: Zee5.2022.
    Newswire.ca

  3. SonyLiv






    SonyLiv has cheap plans for high-standard Bollywood content. It has other content, like sports, and some TV channels. However, if you wish to use it from countries besides India, a VPN is required.
